Reception

Our Inns all offer Reception halls for your weddings! We have catering and kitchen access available so that you can either have us prepare the food for the event, or provide it yourself. We have a wide variety of kitchen appliances and trained staff to ensure that your special day has adequate food for the occasion. To inquire about reserving the reception hall call the number of the Inn you're wishing to host your reception at.
