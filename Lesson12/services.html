Reception

Our Inns all offer Reception halls for your weddings! We have catering and kitchen access available so that you can either have us prepare the food for the event, or provide it yourself. We have a wide variety of kitchen appliances and trained staff to ensure that your special day has adequate food for the occasion. We also have staff that are albe to assist with setting up and taking down all the chairs and tables you'll need. To inquire about reserving the reception hall call the number of the Inn you're wishing to host your reception at.

Full-Time Temple Missionary

We may have rooms available for full time missionaries!

If you're interested in one of these rooms, please go to the Contact Us page and add that you're a full time missionary and what hotel you're interested in when you fill out the comment section. We'll contact you about availability as soon as we can!
